Other Projects

Hydrate

Spring 2020

Hydrate is an app that allows users to track their water intake and understand their hydration levels by watering a virtual plant throughout the day.
I was tasked with designing and rebranding an app of my choice as well as develop the interactions for the app. This included analyzing the a task flow and app flow of MyWater and developing a high fidelity wireframe, wire flow, IX flow, interaction guides, an an animated prototype for Hydrate. The programs used for this design were Figma and Adobe After Effects.

NPR

Winter 2020

National Public Radio is an independent, nonprofit media organization that was founded in 1970. Their mission is to create a more informed public. NPR are storytellers that connect across the country to explore the news, ideas, and what it means to be human. As they put it, “NPR makes local stories national, national stories local, and global stories personal” (npr.org).
This case study will be exploring ways to better NPR in reaching their mission via their mobile, tablet, and desktop states of their website.

YLPL

Spring 2020

The Yorba Linda Public Library (YLPL) is a study space, a place of learning, creativity, and a place where people, ranging from children to seniors, can come together to create a unique community. Through observations and by speaking to the staff, three main improvements were found that can be made.
These improvements focus around library patrons understanding when their books have been returned, optimizing their calendar, and having librarians understand when users have turned in their books.
